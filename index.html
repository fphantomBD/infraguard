Frequently Asked Questions

1. Where can I use it ?
Ans : You can install this device in your Residence, Shops, Small Enterprises, Garages, Office or any Closed space that contains valuables.

2. Can I get after sales support ?
Ans : Yes, you can get support from us.

3. Does INFRAguard™ operate in case of power cut ?
Ans :Yes, in the device there is built-in-rechargeable AAA-NI-MH battery to support during a power cut.

4. How can I install this product ?
Ans : Our expert team will help you to install and teach you to use INFRAguard™ at your home, shop etc.

5. Can I control INFRAguard™ when I am outside ?
Ans : Yes, you can control your device when you are away through your mobile phone.

6. Is it a Government approved Device ?
Ans : Yes, this is the first BTRC approved GSM alarm security device in Bangladesh.

7. How does INFRAguard™ work?
Ans : When any unauthorized intrusion like thieves or break-ins are picked up by the motion detectors & window sensors the alarm will be triggered. As a result INFRAguard TM will automatically send SMS and call the preset mobile numbers to make you aware and at the same time provided Indoor and Outdoor Siren will make sound so loudly that it will attract the attention of people in and around the Area. In most of the cases the intruder will abandon his plan and escape. Same alarm will occur if there is smoke in your place.
